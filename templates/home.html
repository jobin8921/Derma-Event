Skin Health Conclave 2025

Join us for an inspiring two-day journey dedicated to promoting skin health, spreading awareness, and embracing wellness. The Skin Health Conclave 2025 brings together leading dermatologists, skincare brands, celebrities, and health enthusiasts under one roof to celebrate the essence of healthy skin and holistic living.

Conclave Timings

  • Dates: May 18 - 19, 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM (Both Days)
  • Venue: The Park Centre, Technopark, Trivandrum
  • Entry: Open to Public | Free Registration

Experience an enriching weekend full of knowledge-sharing, networking, and rejuvenation.

Key Highlights of the Conclave

  • Celebrity Participation: Engage with top influencers from the film and fashion industries promoting skin health awareness.
  • Free Dermatology Clinics: Get personalized skin consultations, expert skin analysis, and customized care advice by leading dermatologists.
  • Workshops & Live Demonstrations: Attend interactive sessions covering skincare routines, new treatments, and cosmetic dermatology advancements.
  • Natural Skincare Exhibits: Explore organic and dermatologist-tested products presented by trusted skincare brands.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with healthcare professionals, beauty experts, and fellow attendees passionate about skincare and wellness.
